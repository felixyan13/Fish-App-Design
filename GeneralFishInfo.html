All about Sharks

This webpage is dedicated to sharks. I will be listing some information about them.

What do sharks eat?

Sharks eat a whole variety of wildlife. This usually depends on the species. Here are commonly eaten prey:

  1. Fish
  2. Seals
  3. Whales

Fun Facts about sharks

Here, an assortment of random facts will be listed:

  • Sharks have a 6th sense to detect electrical signals
  • Sharks must move perpetually or else they will sink
  • Approximately 100 million sharks are killed every year, mostly for their lucrative shark fins
